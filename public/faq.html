Q:What is the job of a service crew?

A Service Crew may be assigned front counter, lobby or kitchen duties. Front counter duties include taking customers' orders and accepting payment. The Service Crew will also have to interact with customers, clear trays and ensure that the lobby is clean. Lastly, the Service Crew will have to cook and assemble orders (putting together a burger with dressing etc.) in the kitchen.

Q:What are the working hours?

We offer flexible hours for our Service Crew depending on the needs of the restaurant and the availability of our Service Crew. The Restaurant General Manager of each store will be able to provide information regarding the working hours needed by the restaurant.
