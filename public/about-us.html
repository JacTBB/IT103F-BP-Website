Background information

Blueprint Studios was founded in January, 2018 by Mr Bob to cater to game companies that needs assets and accessories to be used in their games. Blueprint Studios is a development company that creates 3D Computer-Aided-Design (CAD) models for game companies to buy and used in their own games. Our first physical outlet was established in 8 January 2018, located in Nanyang Polytechnic Block L, L.629. The products and services that Blueprint Studio offers are 3D Computer-Aided-Design (CAD) models and custom made 3D Computer-Aided-Design (CAD) models. However, competition is strong and it is hard to attract companies. Therefore, Mr Bob decided to implement a website to help attract game companies to Blueprint Studios and share more about what Blueprint Studios has to offer. Having a website avoids the hassle of signing business contracts and allows us to reach out to a wider international market. As a result, this cuts down on the cost needed for traditional marketing methods and also provides ample opportunities to collaborate with other video game companies.
